# Meet the Cows — data terms

These terms cover the **data** published with and produced by this project: the compiled field
packs (`packs/…` — `fields.json`, media, documents), the community contributions
(`contributions/…`), and files the app generates from them (such as CUP exports). The source
code is licensed separately — see [LICENSE](LICENSE).

## What you may do freely

Use the data **through the Meet the Cows app** for personal, noncommercial purposes:
browse it, cache it offline on your devices, and export a CUP file for your own navigation
software. Gliding clubs may do the same for their members.

## What requires permission

Anything else — in particular:

- extracting, scraping or bulk-downloading the pack data outside normal app usage;
- redistributing or rehosting the packs or substantial parts of them;
- building datasets, products or services (commercial or not) on top of them.

Ask first: open an issue on [github.com/br0c/meet-the-cows](https://github.com/br0c/meet-the-cows/issues).
Reasonable requests are welcome — the point is knowing where the data goes, not locking it away.

## No warranty — not for navigation

The data is provided as is, without warranty of any kind. It is an offline situational aid and
**must not be used as a primary navigation or flight-planning source**. Always verify against
current official publications (AIP/VAC/NOTAM) and local knowledge before flight.
